Chop Suey

A classic and hugely popular Filipino recipe, Chopsuey is a stir-fry dish made of mixed egetables in a thickened and perfectly seasoned white or light brown sauce with meat like pork, chicken, and shrimp added to make it substantial. Serve with rice for a tasty, wholesome and complete meal!


Ingredients:

  • ½ cup of water
  • 4 tbsp cornstarch
  • 2 tbsp Fish sauce or Soy Sauce
  • 2 tbsp Oyster Sauce
  • Salt and pepper
  • Red Bell Pepper
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Carrots
  • Broccoli
  • Cauliflower
  • Cabbage
  • Boiled Quail Egg
  • Chicken, Pork, Shrimp, Chicken Liver (meat of your choice)

How to cook?

  1. Heat oil in a pan.
  2. Pan fry the meat of your choice until light brown, set aside.
  3. Sauté the onion. Add garlic and continue to sauté.
  4. Add the meat.
  5. Add Soy sauce/Fish Sauce and Oyster Sauce.
  6. Pour water, Let boil, Cover and cook in medium heat for 15 minutes.
  7. Add cauliflower, carrots, bell peppers, Stir.
  8. Add cabbage. Toss. Cover and cook for 5 to 7 minutes.
  9. Add cornstarch diluted in water.
  10. Add Boiled Quail Eggs.
  11. Transfer to a serving plate. Share and Enjoy!!
